FAQ¶

  • How do I get a starter?

    See Starting your journey

  • How do I use <X>?

    Check if there is a hotkey you can assign for <X> in the controls section of the minecraft options.

  • I died, where did my pokemobs go?

    On death, your pokemobs should go to the PC. You can usually find a PC in each PokeCenter, or you can craft one yourself.

  • I checked my PC, my pokemobs didn’t go there!

    Sometimes your mob might not have recalled when you died. In this case, you need to go back to where you left it, and then it should either still be there or be recalled when you get back.

  • My pokemobs still aren't there!

    Something may have gone wrong, in this case, run /pokecube restore give @p, and then click on the name of the mob in chat. You may need to click on the [] around the name rather than the text itself.

  • Where does <X> spawn?

    Check in the PokeWatch. You can search for <X> in there, and then it can give a general idea of where to search. You may also need to search for lower evolutions instead.

  • I went to where the Pokewatch said to go, but still cannot find it!

    The initial location information does not fully account for extra conditions, including the time of day and required subbiomes. You may need to search around the general area until the mob appears in the Local Spawns page.

  • I tried to catch <X>, but it didn’t go into the pokecube!

    If <X> is a special or legendary pokemob, it may have capture requirements. In this case, check Legends and Quests for more information.
